Adventure Awaits You!

Paddle & Hike

Raft in gentle water to the trailhead. This is a great spot for a picnic (not provided). The trail will take you up Summit Hill and back to the rafts where you will conclude the trip five miles down the river.

Family Fun

This is gentle ride for the whole family. Hop out for a swim. Make the kids paddle. Enjoy conversation. Everyone will enjoy it!
*This trip is eligible for the family reunion special.

Half Day Adventure

This trip starts with a bang on our mid-level rapids. We break for a lunch (provided) on shore and conclude with a gentle, scenic ride. *Let us know of dietary restrictions.

Los Dientes del Diable

All hands on deck! Everyone's help is needed as we battle the rapids for a whopping 2 hours! You'll finished tired, satisfied, and with stories to tell about your escape from "the devil's teeth"!

Extreme Expedition

Geronimo! This is one hour of the most intense rafting you will ever experience. Jagged rocks, sudden drops, and even waterfalls are some of the perils you will face. Be advised that a minimum fitness level is required to survive!
